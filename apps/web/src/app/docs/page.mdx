remediate is a single `<Remediate />` component. it captures everything the user shows you, bundles it into one payload, and hands it to your code. you decide what to do with it — call a backend, forward to an integration, or log it.

there is no hosted service required. bring your own backend, pick an integration formatter, and ship.
